About JKIPM

The Institute is located at NH-91 just only on the Railway Crossing Near Village Agwal about 5 km from Khurja and 80 km from New Delhi and 50km from Noida and is duly connected by rail and road.

The campus has been designed in a pollution free complex. All type basic amenities have been provided and the academic complex provides complete requirement laboratories, rooms, tutorial room, common room for boys and girls and seminar halls. The building is well furnished and cross ventilated.

J.K.I.P.M is well approved by AICTE (All India Council of Technical Education) New Delhi, and affiliated to Abul Kalam Technical University,Lucknow to run B. Pharmacy four years regular course In take of 60 seats have been approved by Pharmacy Council Of India.The Institute also approved by Technical Education Board,Lucknow and PCI,New Delhi To Run D.Pharma Two Years regular course from 2019-20 session. JKIPM provides the quality of Education and facilities to the students in the college. The students have also engaged the opportunity they got and have shown excellent results in the University examination implore top position in the university merit list.
